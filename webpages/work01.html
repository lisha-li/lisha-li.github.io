Description

Winter is a mobile application that monitors your connected smart kitchen appliances anywhere through WIFI or 3G. It learns your cooking habits overtime and predicts your preferences then recommend recipes based on what ingredients are available on hand.

This is a team project for DECO2200 - Interaction Design at the University of Sydney.
