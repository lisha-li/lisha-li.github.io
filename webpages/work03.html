Date: 2014

Type: Concept design for a Wearable App

Involvement: Research, Design


Description

Identifier is designed for wearable technology such as smart glasses. The app will allow users to identify any restaurants nearby as well as search for restaurants within walking distance. The main goal of this app is to quickly and efficiently find a place to dine which suits both the user's needs and wants.

This is a group project for DECO2010 - Designing Social Media at the University of Sydney.
